About 2 Die 4 Costumes

Hello!! My name is Bahar Carmichael and I am the owner of 2 Die 4 Costumes. I started my company because my daughter became interested in dance at a very young age. Designing costumes became something I did while waiting for her to get out of dance class. I started making her solo costumes, then moved onto duos and groups for others in the studio. Before long, business was booming with orders from all over the country. I hand make each costume using the finest materials to include Swarovski crystals. Each costume is custom designed based on the style, materials, and colors specified by YOU!
